Overview

Data We Collect

When you subscribe to our mailing list, we collect your email address. This data is processed by Kit (formerly ConvertKit), our email service provider based in the United States.

We use this solely to send you updates about our products and company news. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in every email.

Website Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics service. Plausible does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and does not track you across websites.

All data is aggregated and anonymous — we see page views and referrer sources, not individual visitors. No consent is required under GDPR/ePrivacy as no personal data is processed. See Plausible's data policy for details.
