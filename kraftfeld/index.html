Kraftfeld

Generative MIDI Drum Sequencer for iPad

Turn one bar into a living performance.

16 steps in. Infinite variations out.

Kraftfeld takes your simple drum pattern and evolves it over time with intelligent fills, phasing, and LFOs. Designed for live EDM performers who want breathing, dynamic drums — without programming 64 patterns.

Demo video coming soon

One Bar, Endless Variation

Punch in a basic beat. Kraftfeld's generative engine shapes it over time — your 1-bar loop sounds like 4, 8, 16, 32, 64 bars.

Intelligent Evolution

Randomized phasing, dynamic fills, per-step LFOs. Every pass through the loop is subtly different. Never static, always musical.

Performance-Ready

Mute, solo, bass sidechaining — all the controls you need on stage. Zero menu diving. Maximum expression.

MIDI Out

Works with any synth, drum machine, or DAW. Kraftfeld generates the notes — you choose the sounds.
